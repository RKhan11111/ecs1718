Eating Habits








A fennec fox diet in the wild is different than when kept in captivity. Moreso, a fennec fox scavenges for other small animals and insects. Little at times do they eat roots or fruits. By eating other animals, these foxes are able to sustain thier appetites as they provide vitamins that fennecs couldn't have gotten from plants alone. A few of those animals are: reptiles, rodents, birds and bird eggs.
